Melbourne Gun Club

The Melbourne Gun Club is located in the picturesque Yarra Valley, about one hours drive east of Melbourne CBD.

We are one of the largest and best equipped clay target shooting clubs in Australasia.

No firearms licence is required as you will be coached and supervised by experienced members of the club who are keen to make sure your time with us is enjoyable and rewarding.

Corporate Packages & Fees

Melbourne Gun Club will be more than happy to tailor packages to client needs upon request. Our standard package offered is as follows:

Up to 10 people is a fixed fee of $1,600
Additional participants are $80.00 per person
Greater than 40 people will require a tailored package
The above pricing includes a minimum of 25 clay targets per person, all ammunition, shotgun, earplugs, coffee, tea and most importantly coaching and mentoring.
